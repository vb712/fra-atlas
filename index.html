About FRA Atlas

Our Mission

The Forest Rights Act Atlas is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline and enhance the management of forest rights claims across India. Our mission is to provide transparent, efficient, and accessible tools for both government officials and forest-dwelling communities.

Key Objectives

  • Digitize and centralize forest rights claim data
  • Provide real-time tracking and status updates
  • Enable data-driven decision making with AI insights
  • Ensure transparency in the claims process
  • Support evidence-based policy making
